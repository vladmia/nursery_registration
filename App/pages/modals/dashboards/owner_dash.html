User Dashboard Guide

Click on any tile on the dashboard to view more details as the tile caption suggests.

More details can be accessed using the navigation menu provided.

To view the nurseries you have registered, click on the Registered Nurseries tile

To view your registered nurseries for which you are yet to apply for certification, click on the Unapplied Nurseries tile

To view your registered nurseries which have been applied for certification, click on the Applied for Certification tile

To view the nurseries which are yet to be inspected after being applied for certification, click on the Awaiting Inspection tile

To view the nurseries which have qualified for certification after inspection, click on the Certified Nurseries tile

To view the nurseries which did not qualify for certification after inspection, click on the Declined Applications tile
