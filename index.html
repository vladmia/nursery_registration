Why get your nursery certified?

The National strategy for the achievement and maintenance of 30% national forest cover by 2050 has created increased demand for tree planting materials for conservation, industrial and domestic needs. The country requires 1 billion tree seedlings planted annually in order to achieve the target. This calls for extensive and intensive tree seedling production in the country from existing and new tree nurseries. The accreditation of tree nurseries is therefore a necessary measure in order to ensure the disbursement of quality tree seedlings for use in this endeavor.
